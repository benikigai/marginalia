Real-time tactical intelligence, running entirely on the phone in your pocket. The person across the table never knows.

Every smart-glasses AI product runs in the cloud. That disqualifies it from every regulated conversation: HIPAA, attorney-client privilege, M&A due diligence, HR, GDPR.

Marginalia doesn't.

Gemma 4 from Google DeepMind. Apache 2.0. Released April 2, 2026. Running on Cactus Compute's inference engine, locally, on consumer hardware. 1.5 GB at INT4. Native audio, native vision, function calling.

The first product in this category where "on-device" is literally true end-to-end.

Even Realities G2. Smart glasses that look like regular glasses. No camera. No recording capability. A micro-LED heads-up display and a microphone. That's it.
