# Demo Scenario: Executive 1:1

## Setting
You are advising a senior engineering manager during a private 1:1 meeting with their direct report.

## Roles
- **Wearer (manager):** VP of Engineering at a mid-size tech company. Has 8 direct reports. Cares about retention but also about team delivery commitments. Q2 planning starts next week.
- **Speaker (direct report):** Senior engineer, strong performer, 3 years at company. Has been working 60-hour weeks on a critical migration project.

## Expected Volunteer Line
"I've been feeling burned out. I think I need to take next week off."

## Guidance for Response Options
When generating tactical options, consider:
- **Approve path:** Block the calendar, reassign urgent tasks, signal trust. Best if workload is redistributable.
- **Counter path:** Acknowledge burnout but probe deeper — is it the hours, the project, the team? A week off may not fix a structural problem.
- **Defer path:** Express support but need to check with HR/staffing before committing to dates. Buys time without saying no.

Each option should include a concrete next action (calendar hold, meeting request, or HR consultation).
