# Marginalia — 5-Minute Demo Script

**Target time: 4:30-4:45**

---

## ACT 1: Frame (60 seconds)

> *Walk to center stage. Put on G2 glasses casually — they look like regular glasses.*

"Hi, I'm Ben. Staff TPM at Meta, AR/VR hardware — I've spent six years at Apple inside Foxconn factories building the products you use every day.

Here's what I learned: the most valuable conversations in business are the ones that can't be recorded. Attorney-client privilege. Doctor-patient confidentiality. M&A deal rooms. Manufacturing IP. These conversations generate billions in value, and cloud AI can't touch them.

**usemarginalia.com**

So this weekend I built Marginalia — the private commentary on every conversation that can't leave the room."

> *Gesture to glasses.* "Let me show you."

---

## ACT 2: Live Demo (2 minutes 45 seconds)

> *Turn to MacBook. Audience sees the MacBook screen projected.*

### 2A: Airplane Mode (15 seconds)

"First — airplane mode. Watch."

> *Toggle iPhone airplane mode visibly (cellular OFF, hotspot stays ON).*
> *Show Little Snitch or nettop on MacBook: zero outbound bytes.*

"Zero bytes leaving this network. Everything you're about to see runs right here."

### 2B: The Conversation (30 seconds)

> *Point to volunteer in audience.*

"[Volunteer name] is going to play my direct report. We're in a 1:1. Go ahead."

> *Volunteer stands and says:*
> **"I've been feeling burned out. I think I need to take next week off."**

> *Pause. Look at the audience.* "Now watch the lens."

### 2C: Options Appear (15 seconds)

> *Point to the projected G2 lens preview or tilt glasses toward camera.*

"Gemma 4 just analyzed that in [X] seconds. On-device. Three options:"

> *Read aloud from the lens:*
> "Approve — block the calendar. Counter — ask about workload. Defer — send a calendar hold."

### 2D: Ring Tap + Calendar (30 seconds)

> *Hold up R1 ring hand.*

"I'm going to approve."

> *Tap R1 ring. Calendar projection on MacBook shows the event appear.*

"Calendar hold. Done. My direct report doesn't know I just got tactical guidance. The conversation stays natural. The AI stayed in the margin."

### 2E: The Line (15 seconds)

> *Pause for effect. Look at audience.*

"Cloud AI writes in the main text. **Marginalia writes in the margin.**"

> *Beat.*

---

## ACT 3: Company (75 seconds)

> *Advance to verticals — show usemarginalia.com or slide.*

"The demo you just saw is one product. The company is eight products.

Every high-stakes profession gets a fine-tuned Marginalia trained on their domain's privileged data:

- **Marginalia Counsel** for attorneys — trained on deposition transcripts and privilege-protected communications
- **Marginalia Clinical** for physicians — trained on clinical dialog and differential diagnosis workflows
- **Marginalia Diligence** for deal teams — trained on valuation frameworks and MNPI-sensitive vocabulary
- **Marginalia Floor** for manufacturing — trained on equipment manuals and safety procedures

Each one ships through vertical-specific partnerships and compliance certifications that take competitors 12 months to replicate."

> *Pause.*

"I'm starting with manufacturing because I spent six years inside Foxconn. I have the Day One distribution nobody else does.

I'm leaving Meta on August 15th.

I want a YC interview.

**usemarginalia.com**. Thank you."

## Fallbacks (memorize these)
- **G2 audio fails:** Use MacBook mic. Say: "For audio clarity in this room, I'm using the laptop mic — the glasses mic works the same way."
- **Inference slow (>6s):** Fill with: "On an M4 Max, this takes about [X] seconds. On iPhone 17, it's faster with the Neural Engine."
- **Inference fails:** Server is in dummy mode. Pre-loaded response appears. Don't mention it.
- **Ring doesn't work:** Tap temple of glasses instead. Or say: "I'm tapping to select option 1" and click on MacBook.
- **Volunteer flubs the line:** Repeat it yourself: "Let me paraphrase what they said: 'I'm burned out and need next week off.'"
- **Calendar doesn't appear:** Say: "The calendar hold is created — you'd see it on your phone notification." Move on.
