Why On-Device Changes Everything

Cloud AI can't serve regulated industries. Attorney-client privilege, HIPAA, material non-public information, classified environments — these conversations cannot leave the room. Marginalia is the only AI that's legally deployable where it matters most.

▣

Privileged Training Data

Fine-tuned on domain-specific corpora under BAA. Our models know what a deposition sounds like, what a differential diagnosis workflow looks like. Cloud LLMs never will.

◇

On-Device Personalization

Your model learns your voice, your style, your vocabulary over months — and that adaptation never leaves your hardware. Switch devices, lose nothing. Switch products, lose everything.

Compliance Certifications

SOC 2, HIPAA BAA, HITRUST, FedRAMP. Each certification is a 6-12 month barrier to entry that competitors must rebuild from scratch.
